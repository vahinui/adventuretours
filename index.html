MOUNTAIN BIKING

From single track in the summer to fat-tire biking when the snow falls, Montana offers unmatched trails and undiscovered country for mountain bikers of all levels. Many communities feature trailheads right outside your door. So hop on a bike and take a ride through spectacular scenery (hint: finish your ride with a stop at the local brewery to swap stories with other bikers).
